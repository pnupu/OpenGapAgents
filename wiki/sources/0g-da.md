## Key Points

0G DA is a data availability module for publishing data blobs through redundant encoding, DA node verification, aggregated signatures, and onchain confirmation. It is most relevant when a system needs scalable data availability guarantees for larger appchain/rollup-style data.

## Relevance To OpenGap

Likely out of MVP.

Possible later use:

- publish larger batches of interaction traces as DA blobs
- provide availability proofs for public benchmark datasets
- support a rollup/appchain version of OpenGap where generated docs improvements are batched

## MVP Decision

Do not depend on DA for the first hackathon demo unless 0G sponsor feedback specifically asks for it.
