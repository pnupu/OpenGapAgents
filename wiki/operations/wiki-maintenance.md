Status: active  
Updated: 2026-04-25  
Links: [Source ingestion checklist](source-ingestion-checklist.md), [LLM wiki pattern](../concepts/llm-wiki-pattern.md)

## Update Rules

- Read `wiki/index.md` before answering broad project questions.
- Keep source notes separate from project decisions.
- When a source changes, update the source page and every derived page.
- When a decision changes, append to `wiki/log.md`.
- Prefer small pages with strong cross-links over giant notes.

## Weekly Lint Questions

- Are any 0G docs claims stale?
- Do we still target the correct prize track?
- Is the MVP still demoable in under 3 minutes?
- Do we have one full loop working?
- Does each protocol integration have a visible proof point?
