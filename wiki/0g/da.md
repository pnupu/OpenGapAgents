## What It Does

0G DA provides decentralized data availability for blobs. It is designed for systems that need scalable publication and availability guarantees.

## OpenGap Decision

Do not include DA in the first MVP unless required by sponsor feedback. The self-improving docs loop already has enough meaningful 0G integration through Compute, Storage, and Chain.
